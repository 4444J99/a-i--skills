# Workflow Integration: Brand Guidelines

This document describes how `brand-guidelines` integrates with other skills in the Career Development ecosystem.

## Related Skills

| Skill | Relationship | When to Invoke |
|-------|--------------|----------------|
| `cv-resume-builder` | **Downstream** | Apply brand voice to resume |
| `portfolio-presentation` | **Downstream** | Apply identity to portfolio |
| `networking-outreach` | **Downstream** | Apply messaging to outreach |
| `interview-preparation` | **Downstream** | Consistent narrative in interviews |

## Prerequisites

Before invoking `brand-guidelines`, ensure:

1. **Self-assessment complete** - Know your strengths and values
2. **Target audience identified** - Who you want to reach
3. **Differentiation clear** - What makes you unique

## Handoff Patterns

### To: cv-resume-builder

**Trigger:** Brand ready for resume application.

**What to hand off:**
- Voice and tone guidelines
- Value proposition statement
- Key differentiators
- Positioning language

**Expected output from resume:**
- Resume with consistent voice
- Summary reflecting positioning
- Language aligned with brand

### To: portfolio-presentation

**Trigger:** Brand ready for portfolio application.

**What to hand off:**
- Visual identity elements
- Verbal identity guidelines
- Thematic structure
- Positioning context

**Expected output from portfolio:**
- Visually cohesive portfolio
- Project narratives in brand voice
- Consistent presentation

### To: networking-outreach

**Trigger:** Brand ready for networking.

**What to hand off:**
- Elevator pitch
- Key talking points
- Professional tone
- Value proposition

**Expected output from networking:**
- Consistent messaging
- Authentic connections
- Clear asks

### To: interview-preparation

**Trigger:** Brand guides interview narrative.

**What to hand off:**
- Core narrative themes
- Value proposition
- Key stories to emphasize
- Tone for responses

**Expected output from interview prep:**
- "Tell me about yourself" aligned with brand
- Consistent story selection
- Authentic presentation

## Integration Checklist

Before completing brand guidelines, verify:

- [ ] Unique value proposition defined
- [ ] Target audience clear
- [ ] Voice and tone documented
- [ ] Key themes identified
- [ ] Visual identity elements (if applicable)
- [ ] Elevator pitch crafted
- [ ] Differentiators articulated
- [ ] Authentic to who you are

## Common Scenarios

### New Career Starter

1. **Brand Guidelines:** Define emerging professional identity
2. **Portfolio Presentation:** Curate first projects
3. **CV Resume Builder:** Entry-level resume

### Career Transition

1. **Brand Guidelines:** Reposition for new target
2. **Portfolio Presentation:** Reframe relevant work
3. **Networking Outreach:** New industry connections

### Senior Executive

1. **Brand Guidelines:** Executive presence definition
2. **Portfolio Presentation:** Leadership track record
3. **Interview Preparation:** Executive narrative

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Solution |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| Generic positioning | Doesn't differentiate | Find unique angle |
| Inauthentic voice | Hard to maintain | Be genuinely you |
| Inconsistent messaging | Confuses audience | Document and apply consistently |
| Static brand | Becomes outdated | Regular refinement |
