# Research Artifact Templates

## Executive Summary Template

```markdown
# [Topic] Executive Summary

## Context
[2-3 sentences on why this matters]

## Key Findings
1. **[Finding 1]**: [One sentence explanation]
2. **[Finding 2]**: [One sentence explanation]
3. **[Finding 3]**: [One sentence explanation]

## Recommendation
[1-2 sentences on recommended action]

## Key Risks
- [Risk 1]
- [Risk 2]

## Next Steps
1. [Action item]
2. [Action item]

---
*Prepared by: [Name] | Date: [Date] | Sources: [Count] reviewed*
```
